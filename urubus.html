Top Destinations

Punta del este

Heavenly destination for tourists from all over the world. Beach, majestic nature, nightly attractions and ostentatious architecture.

Colonia

A picturesque and beautiful colonial style city. Declared by UNESCO as Patrimony of Humanity; it´s only about 50 km from Buenos Aires.

Salto

Located in the north of the country and facing the argentine city of Concordia, it has an important tourist attraction all year round thanks to its thermal centers.

Punta del Diablo

A beautiful Uruguayan coastal town 45 km from the border with Brazil (Chuy). A place that balances tranquility and nature, rest and adventure.
